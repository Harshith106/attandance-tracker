How It Works

This calculator helps you determine how many classes you can safely skip this week without dropping below your desired attendance percentage.

The formula used is: Bmax = Floor(A + W - (D/100) × (T + W))

Where:

  • T = Total classes held so far
  • A = Classes you've attended so far
  • D = Desired attendance percentage
  • W = Classes per week
  • Bmax = Maximum classes you can bunk
